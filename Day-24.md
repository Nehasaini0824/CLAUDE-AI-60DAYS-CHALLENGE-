Day 24 of #60DayClaudeAIChallenge

We're changing that with Career Platform India — a placement-guaranteed career transformation platform built around one promise: you pay only after you get placed.
Here's what makes it different:
✅ 8-week structured curriculum mapped to real job descriptions
✅ 1:1 industry mentors with hiring authority — not just content
✅ ISA model — 15% of salary × 12 months, only after placement
✅ Live placement dashboard — fully transparent, verified outcomes
✅ AI resume builder + mock interviews built in
Our 30-day MVP goal: 20 paying students. 60% placed in 90 days.
The ₹10,000 Cr+ market is wide open. Incumbents like upGrad and Scaler have funding — but they haven't cracked personalised mentorship or transparent placement guarantees. That's our window.
The strategy is clear: validate first, then scale. Place the first 20. Build proof. Raise.
