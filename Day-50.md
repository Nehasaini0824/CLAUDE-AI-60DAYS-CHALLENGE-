🚀 Day 50/60 — Building an AI that Doesn't Improve Your Resume… It Challenges It. 

Halfway to a major milestone! 🎉

For Day 50 of #60DayClaudeAIChallenge, I designed Defend Your Experience—an AI-powered interview preparation application that goes beyond resume reviews.

Instead of suggesting better wording, it acts like a skeptical interviewer and challenges every claim on your resume until you can confidently defend it.

Screenshot 




💡 What makes it different?

🔹 Extracts every meaningful claim from your resume
🔹 Simulates HR, Technical, and Hiring Manager interview rounds
🔹 Asks adaptive follow-up questions based on your previous answers
🔹 Identifies weak, vague, and unsupported claims
🔹 Tracks confidence and defense progress in real time
🔹 Generates a personalized Defense Report with improvement suggestions
🔹 Stores session history for continuous practice


This project is built around one idea:

A strong resume gets you shortlisted. Your ability to defend it gets you hired.

Designing this challenged me to think beyond prompts and focus on creating an AI experience that feels like a real interview rather than a static questionnaire.

Every day of this challenge is teaching me that AI isn't just about generating answers—it's about building experiences that help people grow.
