✅ Customer Journey Mapping
✅ Buying Triggers & Objections

✅ Pricing Hypothesis
✅ Risk Assessment
✅ 30-Day MVP Roadmap
✅ Founder Action Plan
✅ PMF & Readiness Scores
✅ Final Demand Signal Verdict

The goal is simple:
Build what customers actually need before investing significant time and money.
