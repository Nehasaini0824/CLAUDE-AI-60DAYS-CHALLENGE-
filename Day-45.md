Day 45/60 #60DayClaudeAIChallenge 🚀

Today I built an AI-Powered Decision Report Generator with Claude AI.

Most people make important career and life decisions based on emotions, assumptions, or opinions from others. This project turns uncertainty into a structured decision-making framework.

🔍 What the app does:
✅ Identifies the real decision behind the dilemma
✅ Builds the strongest case for each option
✅ Exposes hidden assumptions and cognitive biases
✅ Creates a weighted Decision Matrix (70-point scoring model)
✅ Runs a Premortem Analysis to predict failure risks
✅ Generates a 7-Day Test Plan before committing
✅ Delivers a clear verdict with reasoning
✅ Creates shareable decision summary cards


screenshot



💡 Key Learning:
The best decisions aren't about finding certainty. They're about reducing uncertainty through structured thinking.

Instead of asking:
❌ "What's the right choice?"

Ask:
✅ "What evidence would make this choice obvious?"

This challenge is teaching me that AI can be more than a content generator—it can act as a thinking partner for better decision-making.
