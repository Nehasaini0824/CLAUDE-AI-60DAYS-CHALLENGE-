🚀 Day 25 of #60DayClaudeAIChallenge

🦈 Built an AI-powered Shark Tank simulator — and got told to "Come Back Later" on my first pitch.
I created Shark Tank AI (powered by Claude) — a tool where founders pitch their startup idea to 4 AI investors, each with a distinct personality:

💼 Marcus Rivera (VC) — grills you on market size & scalability
🚀 Priya Kapoor (Serial Founder) — pushes on execution & team
🙋 Jordan Lee (Customer Advocate) — challenges real-world usefulness
😇 Ethan Blackwood (Angel) — scrutinizes profitability & unit economics


I tested it with CareerLaunch India — a career platform for college students — scored 63/100 and got the brutal verdict: "The concept needs more validation before the sharks are comfortable writing a check."

The AI doesn't go easy on you. And that's exactly the point.
What it does:
✅ Simulates real investor Q&A pressure
✅ Scores your pitch dynamically
✅ Generates a downloadable report
✅ Tracks scores on a live leaderboard
Sometimes the most valuable feedback is a rejection with a reason. 💡
