🚀 **Day 51 of #60DayClaudeAIChallenge**

Today, I designed the concept for an **AI Brand Analytics Dashboard**—a modern platform that transforms social media metrics into clear, actionable business insights for small businesses and startups.

Instead of overwhelming users with endless charts, the focus is on helping them answer one question:

**"What should I do next to grow my brand?"**

✨ **Key highlights:**
📊 Brand Health Score & KPI Overview
📈 Interactive performance visualizations
🤖 AI-generated business insights & recommendations
⚠️ Smart alerts for performance changes
🏆 Top-performing content analysis
📄 Export-ready reports for stakeholders

This project is being planned as a polished SaaS-style product that showcases data storytelling, business intelligence, and user-centric design while staying achievable within a 10-day capstone.

Building products isn't just about displaying data—it's about turning data into decisions.

Looking forward to refining this concept and bringing it to life over the coming days.
