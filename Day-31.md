🚀 Day 31 of #60DayClaudeAIChallenge 

Today, I challenged myself to build an AI Supply Chain Control Tower—an interactive operations simulation that puts the player in the role of a Head of Operations responsible for managing a global supply chain.

What I built:

✅ Real-time KPI dashboard (Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, Inventory Health, Transportation Efficiency, Operating Cost, Revenue Protected & Score)

✅ Dynamic operational alerts such as:
🚢 Port Congestion
🚚 Truck Breakdown
🏭 Factory Machine Failure
📦 Warehouse Stockout
🌦 Weather Disruption
📈 Demand Spike
🚨 Supplier Delay

✅ Multiple decision options for every disruption:

Expedite Shipment
Use Backup Supplier
Reroute Trucks
Increase Production
Transfer Inventory
Approve Air Freight
Delay Decision
Ignore

✅ Intelligent game mechanics where:

Correct decisions improve KPIs and score.
Poor decisions increase costs and reduce customer satisfaction.
Some actions create delayed consequences, just like real-world operations.
